Managing Connections (Edges)

Connecting nodes is easy, but sometimes you need to reorganize them. Here is how you can manage your connections:

1. Quick Delete (Button)
Hover your mouse over any connection line. A small × button will appear. Click it to immediately remove the connection.
2. Selection & Keyboard Delete
Click directly on a connection line to select it. Once selected (it will turn blue), you can press the Delete or Backspace key on your keyboard to remove it.
3. Reconnecting (Update)
You don't have to delete an edge to change its target! Click and drag the end of a connection (where it enters the target node) and drop it onto a different node to update the connection.

Node Tips

  • Right-Click (Context Menu): Right-click a node to see more options (coming soon!).
  • Selection: Click a node to see its properties in the right panel.
  • Multiple Selection: Drag a selection box over multiple nodes to move them all at once.
